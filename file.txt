Backend Folder Structure:
        
        node modules
        src:
            -controller --- LOGIC 
            -dbconfig   --- DATABASE CONFIGURATION/CONNECTIVITY
            -middleware --- BRIDGE B/W routes and controller  ["/login" , middleware1 , middleware2  , loginController]
            -models  ---    SCHEMA OF THE DATABASE AND TABLES 
            -routes  --- ALL USER AND ADMIN ROUTES
            -uploads ---  FOLDER FOR UPLOADING AND STORING IMAGES, PDF , XLSX , & DIFFERENT FILES
            -utils  --- ApiError and ApiResponse files 
            -app.js
            -index.js
        .env --- PROTECTED CREDENTIALS
        package.json  --- DEPENDENCIES 
        package-lock.json
